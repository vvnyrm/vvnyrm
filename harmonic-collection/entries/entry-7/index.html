The Story within
Music Production

One of the most significant singer/song writer of this century, Billie Eilish,
is no doubt the idol for countless teenagers and young adults.
However, her contribution to the music industry is not only about her popularity,
but also the amazing making and production process behind all of her tracks.
Pretty much a two-man-team, Billie and her older brother, Finneas O'Conell,
has been recording and producing their music in Finneas' bedroom at their family home.
That is where the dream AND reality started.
Billie's music is iconic. It is within her voice and the way she portrays herself
in her music. The heavily effected vocals, harmonies, and sound effects such as
an Easy-Bake oven, dentist drill, and audience reastion, are all present in her songs.
If you have not already, give her music a try and you will be garenteed a journey.
A journey through emotions, actions, and exquisite techniques.
Now, it is your time to pick a path.
