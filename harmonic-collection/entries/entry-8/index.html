Special Effects

Today, our music and sound industry is not just a minor category
that is overshadowed by others. With the help of technology, recording qualities,
editing techniques and sound strategies have all gone to another level.
In popular music and sound tracks in movies, you would often hear special
effect sounds here and there. For example, cooking noises are sometimes
used as rain sounds, and food packages can often produce metal sounds.
